Travel

MTR is Hong Kong's fast, efficient, and clean subway system launched in 1979. It connects key districts, the airport, and even mainland China. Known for its punctuality and ease of use, it's essential to daily life and tourism alike.
Octopus Card

A rechargeable smart card used across Hong Kong transit (MTR, buses, ferries, etc.) and even in shops.

Monthly Pass

A pass loaded onto your Octopus card permitting unlimited travel between specified stations over one month.

Single-Day Pass

A pass that gives you unlimited travel on MTR, Light Rail, and MTR Bus for 24 hours from the first ride.

One-Way Pass

A pay per trip pass. Fare rises the farther you travel.
